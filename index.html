New to Digital Humanities?

This class is designed for students (and a teacher) who are entirely new to Digital Humanities.

Students who have been hesitant or reluctant to use digital methods in their studies—myself included—are especially welcome.

Course context

American literature through three digital lenses

An undergraduate American literature course primarily for English majors with little or no CS or DH background.

Each week, the class reads one short story and experiments with frequency and context, words in relation, and contextual search.
